Herb · Culinary & Medicinal
🌿

Melissa Officinalis

Lemon balm. Harvest before flowering for highest volatile oil content. Infuse fresh in honey or dry for tisane. Calming, digestive, antiviral.

Flower · Dye & Medicinal
🌼

Calendula Officinalis

Pot marigold. Deep orange petals yield warm yellow-gold dye with alum mordant. Infused oil for skin. Dead-head daily for continuous bloom.

Root · Medicinal
🌱

Valeriana Officinalis

Valerian. Harvest roots in autumn after three years. Tincture in 40% alcohol. Strong sedative action — work with caution and proper dosing.
