Gross Revenue Surpasses $12.8 Billion — Fourth Quarter

Capital Structure · December 31, 2025

Debt-to-Equity Held at 1.4× Despite Expansion Cycle

The treasury team executed three opportunistic bond buybacks in Q3 and Q4, reducing weighted average cost of debt by 42 basis points. Revolving credit facility headroom remains at $3.2 billion. Net leverage, adjusted for operating lease capitalization under IFRS 16, is 1.38× trailing twelve months EBITDA — a 12-year low for the firm.
